Welcome to The Legend of Link: A Zelda RPG

Link is lost!

Zelda must find Link before the monsters get him. Link cannot cry for help, so Zelda must go on her own.

Game Play

Overview | This is point-and-click turn-based game. You are Zelda, and have to survive until you find Link. You will fight monsters and collect items on the way!

Zelda | Zelda has health, max life, attack, and defence. All of her stats can change from fighting monsters or picking up items. Zelda can move to an adjacent square (up, down, left right) by clicking on the square. Zelda will consume items or fight monsters on the square she moves to.

Items | Zelda can find the following items to help her search. She consumes the item by moving into its square.

  • Weapon: Increases Attack. Zelda can hold multiple weapons and increase her attack with each pickup.
  • Shield: Increases Defence. Zelda can only hold one shield. She will trample a shield she finds if she is already holding one.
  • Food: Increases Health. Up to maximum health.
  • Heart Container: Increases Maximum Health. Full heart containers only!
  • Treasure: Provides a random Weapon, Shield, Food, or Heart Container.

Monsters | Monsters have health, attack, and defence. They can pick up weapons and shields and will trample all other items. They will fight Zelda and Link but will not fight each other. Monsters move randomly to an adjacent square (up, down, left right).

Link | When Link shows up, he will automatically defeat monsters that attack him and trample any items he finds.

Turn Order | Turn order is Zelda -> Monsters -> Link. Monsters will move in the order they appeared.

Movement | Zelda moves every turn. Monsters move every other turn. Link moves every other turn

Winning and Losing | You win if you move Zelda to Link's tile once he appears. You lose if Zelda's health goes to 0.

Good luck, and watch out for bees!
